RULES & REGULATIONS

  • The competition is open only for all Arya Vaishya community members.
  • There will be only three formats in the competition, i.e. Solo dance, Couple dance, duet dance. One Contestant can participate only in one of these patterns.
  • There are only two groups: Group A: DOB between 13July 2001 to 13 July 2010 Group B: DOB before 13July 2001.
  • There is no separate Prize category for Couple dance or Duet dance.
  • In case of Duet dance, age of the elder participant will be considered for the Age group (Note: you cannot change your partner in further rounds) and Prize will be given as shared prize with your partner.
  • Registration fee is Rs. 400 for Group A(9-18 Years) and Rs. 500 for Group B (18 years +).
  • The last date of registration is 24 June 2019.
  • The participant needs to send their dance song on av.danceclub@gmailcom in MP3 format and also needs to bring in USB stick and mobile at the venue.
  • Maximum 60 participants will be allowed from Each group for audition round.
  • Total 21 contestants will be qualified from Each group for the second round (Semi-final).
  • Total 12 contestants will be qualified from Each group for the third round (Grand finale).
  • Participation is allowed for contestants of any city but the Venue for all three rounds will be at Pune only. Note:- Contestants from Outside Pune can claim traveling allowance at actual* for only Final round. *T & C apply Max allowances Rs 1500/- on submission of appropriate proofs. DOJ on traveling ticket to Pune should be after 3rd June 2019 and return ticket DOJ should be after 3 days of the final round.
  • Contestants outside of Pune who cannot travel to Pune for the audition round can send YouTube video link of their dance performance on av.danceclub@gmailcom by 25th June 2019.
  • The participant should be available at the auditorium for respective rounds on the allotted time, otherwise, the registration will be cancelled for further rounds.
  • Voting system after Semifinal: 25% weightage will be given to the voting (but only one vote for each group is allowed from any mobile number), 75% weightage is reserve for judges decision.
  • The voting line will be closed after 2 days of Semifinal and result of Semifinal will be disclosed on 9th July 2019.
  • Appreciation Certificate will be awarded to each participant who appeared for the audition and Semifinal. A small memento/souvenir will be awarded to all other finalists.
  • The Certificate collection is the own responsibility of the participant after the respective round.
  • Max. timing for the performance: audition round-2minutes, Semifinal-3minutes, final- 4minutes.
  • The judge's panel will be Professional dance choreographer outside our community.
  • Judges decision will be the final decision.
  • Result of the competition will be declared after final round dance performance on the same day. Prize distribution ceremony will be held thereafter.
  • Snacks will be available at a food stall on chargeable basis at the venue.
  • The committee has rights for alteration /correction in the above plan/rules due to any unavoidable reasons if any.
  • All the above rules are binding for each participant.
